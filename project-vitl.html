The Problem

Modern medicine is reactive. You get symptoms, you get treated. But the data to predict, prevent, and personalise health outcomes has existed for years - it just hasn't been made accessible or intelligible to patients.

VITL was founded on the belief that health optimisation shouldn't be reserved for elite athletes or Silicon Valley executives. A doctor-led, data-first approach to longevity and vitality should be within reach for anyone willing to engage with their own biology.

What I Built

Starting from a blank page, I designed and built the entire VITL brand and digital presence - from visual identity through to the client-facing website and booking infrastructure.

  • Full visual identity: logomark, type system, colour palette, brand guidelines
  • Website design and build on Squarespace with custom CSS and layout logic
  • Content strategy across homepage, services, testing, and about pages
  • AI-augmented copy and content workflows to compress production timelines
  • Online booking integration and consultation flow design
  • Photography art direction for brand imagery

Areas of Expertise on the Platform

VITL combines nutrigenomics, gut microbiome analysis, and comprehensive metabolic lab work to map each client's unique health blueprint. The platform covers:

Weight Loss Genetics Body Composition Women's Health Gut Health Metabolism Toxins Testing Weight Gain

The Three-Stage VITL Method

The service was designed around a clear, repeatable journey that moves clients from data to action to optimisation:

  • Stage 1 - Test: Comprehensive three-stage testing and initial consultation to baseline your biology
  • Stage 2 - Decode: One-on-one consultation to decode your health data and identify root causes
  • Stage 3 - Optimise: Personalised plan with ongoing support, re-assessment, and continuous refinement

Design Principles

The brand needed to feel clinical enough to command trust, but warm enough to feel human. I landed on a palette of deep charcoals and off-whites with gold accents - authoritative without being cold. Typography leans on sharp serifs for credibility, paired with clean sans-serifs for legibility.

Every design decision was made with one question in mind: does this feel like somewhere you'd trust with your body?
